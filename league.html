League of Legends

League of Legends is what is called a MOBA which stands for Multiplayer Online Battle Arena. In league there are two teams of 5 that go against each other and fight to destroy each others nexus. There are 5 different roles in each team and they consist of top lane, mid lane, jungle, bot lane, and support. Both top and mid play in their own lane while support and bot go together. The jungle is much different than the laners because they have their own jungle monsters they have to defeat to get stronger. This is a game I generally enjoy playing with when I'm able to play with friends as its not much fun to play alone. It feels fast paced and can be very rewarding when you are doing well. When things aren't going well though the game can be pretty demoralizing and dreadful to play. I still enjoy playing it though when I'm with friends and bored.
